Shannon Veitch

I am a Masters student working in the Cryptography, Security, and Privacy (CrySP) lab at the University of Waterloo with Professor Doug Stinson.

I received my undergraduate degree in Combinatorics & Optimization at the University of Waterloo. As an undergraduate, I also worked under the supervision of Professor David Jao and Professor Douglas Stebila.
