Shannon Veitch   she/her

I am a doctoral student in the Applied Cryptography Group at ETH Zürich, supervised by Kenny Paterson.

I received my Master's of Mathematics in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, where I worked in the CrySP lab . My thesis was on the topic of secret sharing schemes and was supervised by Doug Stinson. I received my undergraduate degree in Combinatorics & Optimization at the University of Waterloo.
